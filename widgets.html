Basic Functions

  • Sign Up

    • Launch the app from your mobile device and select sign up
    • Enter in your names, college, Email address, phone number and password
    • Confirm the password
    • Hit the create button

  • Login

    • On the login page
    • Enter your email address
    • Enter in your correct password
    • Hit the login Button

  • View Residential Areas

    • Once you logged in, alist of all available residential areas will be displayed on your screen
    • When you click on any of the residential areas on your screen, a list of services offered, a google maps direction as well as booking option will be displayed

  • Booking

    • Click on the booking option
    • Your screen will be redirected to the mobile money services
    • Make payments to book you bed space
